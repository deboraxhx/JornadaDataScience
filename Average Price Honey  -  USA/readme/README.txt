Average price prediction of honey


This project is usefull for my learning about models of regressions and how to made features selections for 
optimal proporse. And in the process it gave me a lot of fun.

- The data are from Kaggle. After beginning, I found more data in the platform;
- Was perform the cleaning and standardization of data, and after performing the concatenate of dataset, 
in addition to check the data consistency;
- EDA was conducted for more learning about the data; 
- We define the baseline of the project, to compare the results of the models. ;
- The model of regression we have use was LinearRegression and RandomForestRegression from Scikit-learn.;
- For the two models i have conduct feature analysis;
